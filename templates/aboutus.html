About Distinction Art Music Center

Our company, Distinction Art Music Center was founded more than 10 years ago in Tai Po. The aim of the business is to provide a platform for people to learn music. People with different age are welcome to learn music in the center. We have also sponsored some events, including Asia Star Piano Competition. The teachers in the center have already gotten grade 8 or above music instrument certification. Moreover, some teachers have ATCL or above certification. Therefore, it is believed that we can provide a better learning experience for customers.

Music Education

We have provided a range of courses for our customers to learn music, including:

  • Piano
  • Violin
  • Flute
  • Guitar
  • Clarinet
  • Music theory, etc.
